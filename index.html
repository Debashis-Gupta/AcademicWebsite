    Background

    This article is about how the well established deep learning model used or assumed effective in usage for medical science especially for diagnostic purpose of several disease can be faced a great adverse effect by small crafted noise called perturbation. This type of attack is widely known as an Adversarial Attack.

    In this study, we investigated the effect of First Gradient Sign Method (FGSM)for radiography images such as Chest X-Ray and CT scan of Covid-19 patient on widely known VGG16 and InceptionV3 models. Different ranged perturbations -nonvisiable and close to visiable- from 0.0009 to 0.01 have been used to see how the models are vulnerable to this attack.To a great extent these models on this dataset have failed to prevent the attack and resulted in misclassification.

    Contribution
    1. Effect of FGSM on Correct Classification

    Both models still can classify some images correctly under attack environment. However, the percentage of confidence reduced for both models. This is due to the crafted noise endeavours to deviate the decision line to fool the model.

    2. Adversary Effect on Models' Accuracy

    FGSM attack outstrips VGG16 model which was failed completely to classifiy the labels. Although Inception-V3 model also showed a great drop in performance, it was better than Vgg16.

    This implies no matter how simple or complexed model one is, it always has a question of utilization in crucial field like medicine.

    3. Iteratic Perturbation Testing on Models

    The following architecture is used to test the models' robustness on iterative approach by updating each time noise variation for every images. Until the model misclassify the input label for different crafted noise the attack continues and stops either on a mislabel or last epsilon.

    Background

    This study shows the effect of Gaussian Noise on adversarial training. In this experiment, we investigated that adding gaussian noise to the inner architecture of widely used deep learning model like ResNet50 can augment the performance of the model against different perturbation during FGSM attack. This approach much more important in medical science for clustering diseases effectively under adversary environment.
